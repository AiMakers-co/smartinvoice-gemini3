# Navigation Guide

This document explains what each page in the dashboard does.

---

## Dashboard
**Path:** `/dashboard`

The main overview page showing key metrics, recent activity, and quick actions.

---

## Banking

Your bank accounts and financial data.

### Accounts
**Path:** `/accounts`

Manage your connected bank accounts. Add new accounts, view balances, archive old accounts. Upload bank statements here.

### Statements
**Path:** `/statements`

Upload and view bank statements (PDF). The AI extracts transactions automatically from uploaded statements.

### Transactions
**Path:** `/transactions`

View all transactions extracted from your bank statements. Filter by date, amount, type, currency.

### Reconciliation
**Path:** `/reconciliation`

Match bank transactions to invoices/bills. The AI suggests matches and you confirm or reject them.

---

## Receivables (A/R)

**Money coming IN to you** - invoices you send to customers.

### Overview
**Path:** `/receivables`

Dashboard view showing unmatched invoices, recent incoming payments (credits), and match suggestions.

### Invoices
**Path:** `/receivables/invoices`

List of invoices you've sent to customers. Upload invoices here. Track which are paid, overdue, or pending.

---

## Payables (A/P)

**Money going OUT from you** - bills you receive from vendors.

### Overview
**Path:** `/payables`

Dashboard view showing unpaid bills, recent outgoing payments (debits), and match suggestions.

### Bills
**Path:** `/payables/bills`

List of bills you've received from vendors. Upload bills here. Track what you owe and due dates.

---

## Tools

Utility tools for document processing.

### PDF to Sheet
**Path:** `/pdf2sheet`

Convert any PDF table to a spreadsheet (Excel/CSV). General-purpose tool, not tied to accounting.

---

## Summary

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Banking** | Your bank data - accounts, statements, transactions, reconciliation |
| **Receivables** | What customers owe YOU (overview + invoices) |
| **Payables** | What YOU owe vendors (overview + bills) |
| **Tools** | General utilities |
